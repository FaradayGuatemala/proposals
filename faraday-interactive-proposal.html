⚠ Before installation day

  • Clear the charging location area and its surroundings — the wall and space where the charger will be mounted, whether that's in the garage, a carport, or an exterior wall. Our electrician needs safe working space.
  • Make sure we can access the electrical panel we'll be installing from — the main panel, or the subpanel if your installation uses one.
  • If the space isn't cleared on installation day, a re-schedule and additional fee may apply.
